How You Can Help

Turn Off Lights
Turn off the lights when you leave a room. Much of the energy in the world is created by burning fossil fuels. A lot of carbon dioxide is let out into the atmosphere from this which increases climate change.
Turn Off Lights
Don't Waste Food
Greenhouse gases are emitted in food landfills and in transporting food. They are also emitted in the production of food, especially in meat products.
Don't Waste Food
Reuse
Do not use single use plastic. Reuse as much as you can!
Reuse
Reduce Your Carbon Footprint
There are many ways to reduce your carbon footprint. You can do it by following all the previous steps. You can also do it by recycling more, having less garbage, carpooling/taking public transit/walking or cycling!
Reduce Your Carbon Footprint
